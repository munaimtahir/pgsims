# PGMS Supervisor Quick Guide

This guide assists clinical supervisors in reviewing postgraduate evaluations and logbooks.

---

## 1. Onboarding & Dashboard
- **Login**: Use the credentials provided by the administrator and update your password on first login.
- **Roster & Workload**: Access `/academics/supervisor-workload` (or `/dashboard/supervisor`) to audit your assigned residents list and count of outstanding review tasks.

---

## 2. Reviewing Evaluations
1. Navigate to `/academics/evaluations` or your workload dashboard.
2. Locate items marked `SUBMITTED` in the list.
3. Review the resident's scores and comments.
4. Select one of the following actions:
   - **Approve**: Confirm completion and lock the record.
   - **Return for Revision**: Enter notes requesting corrections.
   - **Reject**: Decline the submission.

---

## 3. Verifying Logbook Procedures
1. Open `/academics/logbook` or your supervisor workload dashboard.
2. Under pending tasks, click on a submitted clinical entry.
3. Review the procedure description, role performed, complexity, and resident reflections.
4. Input comments and choose **Verify**, **Return**, or **Reject**.

---

## 4. Resident Progress Auditing
- Open `/academics/reports/resident-progress/[resident_id]` for any assigned resident to view their verified procedures summary.
- Export progress report summaries to CSV by clicking **Export CSV**.
