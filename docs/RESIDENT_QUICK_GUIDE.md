# PGMS Resident Quick Guide

Welcome to the PGMS Postgraduate Training Spine system. This guide will help you manage your profile, evaluations, and clinical logbooks.

---

## 1. Onboarding
- **First Login**: Use the username and default password (`pgfmu123`) provided by your administrator.
- **Change Password**: You will be prompted to set a secure password immediately.
- **Complete Profile**: Complete any missing required fields (e.g. email, phone, hospital) before accessing the dashboard.

---

## 2. Dashboard & Progress
- **My Progress**: View your active training spine dates, academic year, and supervisor assignments at `/academics/my-progress` (or `/dashboard/resident`).
- **Data Integrity**: If any required spine field is missing, a compliance warning will notify you to contact the administrator.

---

## 3. Submitting Evaluation Forms
1. Go to `/academics/evaluations` and click **New Evaluation**.
2. Select your current academic period and evaluation template form.
3. Fill out the self-evaluation scores and comments.
4. Click **Submit**. Your evaluation will be placed in your supervisor's review queue.

---

## 4. Submitting Clinical Logbooks
1. Navigate to `/academics/logbook` and click **Log Procedure**.
2. Enter the entry date, clinical title, patient age, gender, and category.
3. Complete the procedure details (name, code, role performed, complexity, and outcome) along with your reflections.
4. Click **Submit** to request verification from your primary supervisor.

---

## 5. Returned / Rejected Submissions
- If a supervisor requests changes on an evaluation or logbook entry, its status changes to `RETURNED`.
- View comments, click **Edit**, update the details, and re-submit.
