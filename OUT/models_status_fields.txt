# Models With Status-like Fields

- academics.StudentProfile: `status`, `status_updated_at`
- analytics.AnalyticsEvent: `status_from`, `status_to`
- attendance.AttendanceRecord: `status`
- attendance.Session: `status`
- bulk.BulkOperation: `status`
- cases.CaseReview: `reviewer`, `status`, `review_date`
- cases.ClinicalCase: `literature_review`, `status`, `reviewed_by`, `reviewed_at`
- cases.HistoricalClinicalCase: `literature_review`, `status`, `reviewed_at`, `reviewed_by`
- certificates.Certificate: `status`
- certificates.CertificateReview: `reviewer`, `status`, `review_date`
- certificates.HistoricalCertificate: `status`
- logbook.HistoricalLogbookReview: `status`, `review_date`, `reviewer`
- logbook.LogbookEntry: `status`, `submitted_to_supervisor_at`
- logbook.LogbookReview: `reviewer`, `status`, `review_date`
- logbook.LogbookStatistics: `submitted_entries`, `average_review_score`, `average_review_time`
- results.Exam: `status`
- rotations.HistoricalRotation: `status`
- rotations.Rotation: `status`
- rotations.RotationEvaluation: `status`
